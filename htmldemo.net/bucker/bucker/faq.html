General Question

  • What is Catchy Bakery?
    • Catchy Bakery is your one-stop baking destination! We specialize in freshly baked cakes, pastries, and breakfast treats and we also sell top-quality baking ingredients, tools, and equipment for home and professional bakers.
  • Where are you located?
    • We’re located at Area 2 shopping complex. You can also order online and have our products delivered right to your doorstep.
  • How do I place an order?
    • You can order directly on our website just browse, add items to your cart, and check out securely. For custom cakes, fill out our “Custom Order” form or call us directly.
  • How far in advance should I place a custom cake order?
    • For custom cakes, please place your order at least 3–5 days in advance. For wedding or large event cakes, we recommend 2 weeks’ notice.
  • Do you offer same-day delivery?
    • Yes! We offer same-day delivery for selected cakes and pastries within [your delivery area]. Cut-off time is usually before 2 PM.
  • Do you sell baking ingredients and tools?
    • Absolutely! We stock everything from flour, chocolate, and fondant to mixers, pans, and decorating tools — perfect for both hobbyists and pros.
  • Are your products freshly baked?
    • Yes, all our baked goods are prepared daily using fresh, high-quality ingredients.
  • Do you offer gluten-free or sugar-free options?
    • Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free and sugar-free cakes and pastries. You can filter by “Dietary Option” on our website.
  • Can I request a special flavor or design?
    • Of course! We love creativity. Share your ideas and we’ll bring your dream cake or pastry to life.
  • What are your delivery charges?
    • Delivery fees depend on your location and order size. The exact fee will be shown at checkout.

Payment & Gift card

  • What payment methods do you accept?
    • We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and cash on delivery (COD) for local orders.
  • Do you offer refunds or exchanges?
    • We do! If there’s an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with your order details and photos.
  • Are online payments secure?
    • Yes. Our website uses secure payment gateways and SSL encryption to protect your data.
  • Is payment required before delivery?
    • Yes, full payment is required to confirm your order. For large custom cake orders, a 50% deposit is accepted, with the balance due before pickup or delivery.
  • Does Catchy Bakery offer gift cards?
    • You can buy a digital gift card for any amount (starting from ₦5,000 / $10). We’ll send you a unique gift card code by email or text, which can be used during checkout.
  • Can I use a gift card online and in-store?
    • Yes, our gift cards can be redeemed both on our website and at our physical shop.
  • Do gift cards expire?
    • No — Catchy Bakery gift cards never expire!
  • Can I get a refund for a gift card?
    • Gift cards are non-refundable once issued, but they can be transferred to someone else easily.
